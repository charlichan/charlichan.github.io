A Music Video(Group Project)

  

General Idea

This is a music video whose original music tells a story about a heartbroken man’s feelings after his breakup. Our group decided to change it into a story about two girls’ friendship.
At the beginning of the MV, I chose a scene of a WeChat message to illustrate the end of the friendship between the two girls instead of explaining the reason. After this scene, I edited different scenes together to explain the feeling of sadness, the two girls are doing the same thing in the same places but they don’t have a connection anymore after the friendship ended. As for the climax part, I put the scene of breaking a bottle to express the emotional outburst, and it’s followed by some close-up scenes of the heroine. In the second part of the MV, I mainly used some scenes about the two friends walking and sitting together, and I also used special effects and montage to make the heroine’s friend disappear. At the end of the MV, the scene returns to the screen of the phone, and the heroine pulls the other girl into the blacklist of WeChat, which I hope corresponds to the opening part and tells the ending of the story.

My Duty

I was the main post-editor of this project. Besides that, I participated in every stage of production with my group members because I really enjoyed it.
