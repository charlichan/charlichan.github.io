An One-minute Video(Personal Project)

  

Introduction

This is a two-dimensional promotion video for my hometown Chengdu. With my self-taught skills on After Effects, I tried my best to show the typical lifestyle and also famous attractions with plane figures, and they were organized by a time line from morning to evening.

Main Elements

Challenge

When I was envisaging this project, I never anticipated that the biggest challenge was making those figures. Due to the particularity of flat design, I found it difficult to present those three-dimensional elements in our real life through simple geometric figures, especially the architectures.
