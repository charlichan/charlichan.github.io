A Music Video(Group Project)

  

General Idea

Since it is a sweet love song with lilting rhyme, we chose a kind of special way to make the music video, with some hand-drawn elements and strong-colored backgrounds. Moreover, we didn’t make any scenes of the whole body of our actresses, but mainly illustrated the theme through the interaction of these two main characters’ hands or other parts of their body.

My Duty

In the production of this MV, I was responsible for the entire post-editing part. Furthermore, during the process of writing scripts and shooting, I also took part in the discussions with other members and helped to make decisions.
During my editing, I first selected the required materials and sorted it according to the lyrics, then used Photoshop to create required graphic materials and finally combined them together with the videos by Premiere.
