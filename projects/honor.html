2022 HONOR Creativity Challenge: 

You are the 1/10000 of the Millennial Generation

(Group Project)

  

The Background

Through research on the domestic smart phone market, using the topic of "university graduation season" as the marketing theme, plan a creative marketing plan for the younger generation of consumers for the Honor 70s series mobile phone, and make a creative video.

The Responsibilities

Use PEST, five forces analysis, SWOT and other analysis methods to complete industry analysis and market research, output a creative marketing plan targeting post-00s, and use a three-minute story short film as a carrier, and independently complete report writing and PPT production

Final Overview
The final project was awarded as the Outstanding Trainee with the 5-person team of the project.
