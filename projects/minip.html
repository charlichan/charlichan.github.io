A WeChat Mini Program(Personal Project)

  

The Concept

With the popularity of Key Opinion Leader in recent years, a large number of youngsters in mainland China have begun to share their opinions on their own WeChat public accounts.

When I was browsing my subscriptions, I noticed that layout design is an important factor for me to choose my favorite subscription and evaluate an account, so I decided to design a simple, portable learning tool for beginners of typesetting.

Logo Design

The first version was just a simple round shape with the name on its center, while in the second version I added two semicircles to simulate paper wrapping.

Then one day when I stared at this logo, I felt that these two small semicircles looked like a mountain and a lake. Therefore, I put a sun above the mountain, which means hope for the beginners.

Challenges

Firstly, the official development tool provided by Tencent was used during the whole coding process of this mini-program. The biggest challenge was adjusting to the differences between this totally new language and the computer language I was familiar with.

Besides, because this mini-program has not been formally promoted, some of the imagined interactive and feedback functions could not be realized.

Final Overview
The final project has three main sections: Study, Review, and Contact. Users can learn new skills from video classes in the Study section and review these skills from graphics and texts in the Review section.
