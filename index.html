ARTIST STATEMENT

Hi! I'm Zhijie Chen, welcome to visit my portfolio.

With thinking about all the things around my life analytically, critically, and independently, I have produced a wide range of media forms step to step from simply imitating to creatively developing. In my art, I tried my best to use the simplest and most common elements to create an immersive experience for the audience to arouse their sympathetic response. Always fascinated by the aesthetics of Wabi-Sabi, I do believe that simplicity and blankness are what every artist should pursue in the end.
